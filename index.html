Projects

Last Escape

Last Escape is a multiplayer game for 2 to 4 players. The players compite to be the first one to escape. To achieve this, they need to look for items and better weapons.

Each game is different as every item is randomly distributed around the map. Also, you can only see the level and enemies if you point them with the flashlight, otherwise, you will only see the walls.

BiOdyssey

BiOdyssey is a space exploration game, based on discoveries. Your objective is to search for planets with new life forms to make your home planet habitable again.

Every aspect of the game, the stars, the planets, the animals, are generated procedurally. As you discover new species and biomes, you can bring them with you to your planet, to customize it as you like.
